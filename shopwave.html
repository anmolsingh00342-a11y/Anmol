Frequently Asked Questions

Contact Us
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express delivery (available in select cities) delivers within 1-2 business days. Orders above ₹999 qualify for free standard delivery.
What is the return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. The item must be in original condition with tags attached.
Are products 100% authentic?
Absolutely! We source all products directly from authorized brand distributors and manufacturers. Every product comes with a brand warranty and authenticity certificate.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking, EMI options, and Cash on Delivery for eligible orders.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your order in real-time from the "My Orders" section in your profile.
